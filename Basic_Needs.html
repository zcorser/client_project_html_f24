Basic Needs Resources

UMSI offers a variety of resources to support students' basic needs, including:

  • Maize & Blue Cupboard
  • Housing Assistance
  • Financial Aid
  • Health & Wellness Services

Maize & Blue Cupboard

The Maize & Blue Cupboard is a student-run food pantry that provides free food and other essentials to members of the University of Michigan community who are facing food insecurity. The pantry is open to all U-M students, faculty, and staff, and offers a variety of non-perishable food items, fresh produce, personal care products, and other essentials. The Maize & Blue Cupboard is committed to promoting food security and reducing hunger on campus by providing nutritious food and resources to those in need.

Emergency Housing Assistance

UMSI provides emergency housing assistance to students experiencing housing insecurity or homelessness. The program offers temporary housing options, financial assistance, and support services to help students secure stable housing.
