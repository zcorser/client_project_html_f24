Maize & Blue Cupboard

The Maize & Blue Cupboard is a student-run food pantry that provides free food and other essentials to members of the University of Michigan community who are facing food insecurity. The pantry is open to all U-M students, faculty, and staff, and offers a variety of non-perishable food items, fresh produce, and other essentials. The Maize & Blue Cupboard is committed to promoting food security and reducing hunger on campus by providing nutritious food and resources to those in need.

How to Schedule Your Maize Cupboard Appointment

Students can schedule an appointment to visit the Maize & Blue Cupboard by visiting the Maize & Blue Cupboard Calendly Scheduling Site. Appointments are available on a first-come, first-served basis and can be scheduled up to two weeks in advance. Students are encouraged to schedule an appointment in advance to ensure that they receive the items they need.
