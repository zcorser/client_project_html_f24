Academic Support at UMSI

UMSI Academic Support

UMSI offers a variety of academic support resources to help students succeed in their studies. These resources include:

  • Programming Tutoring
  • Math Tutoring
  • Peer Tutoring Program
  • Academic Advising

Programming Tutoring

UMSI offers programming tutoring services to help students master complex programming languages and concepts. Programming tutors are available to provide individualized support and guidance to students seeking to improve their programming skills and succeed in their coursework.

Math Tutoring

UMSI provides math tutoring services to assist students in mastering mathematical concepts and techniques. Math tutors are available to work with students on a one-on-one basis to help them develop their math skills and succeed in their coursework.

Peer Tutoring Program

UMSI's peer tutoring program offers students the opportunity to receive academic support and guidance from their peers. Peer tutors are available to work with students on a one-on-one basis to help them master challenging subjects and improve their academic performance.

Academic Advising

UMSI's academic advising team provides personalized guidance to help students navigate their curriculum, select courses, and address any academic challenges or questions they may encounter during their studies. Academic advisors are available to meet with students one-on-one to discuss their academic goals and develop a plan for success.
